Buttons

5-level hierarchy: Primary · CTA · Secondary · Contextual · Text. All action buttons share height 3rem (--btn-height) and radius 0.75rem (--btn-radius). Hover lifts translateY(-2px); active pushes down +1px. Fully interactive — hover and click each one.

Utilities & constraints

.sr-onlyHidden visually, readable by screen readers. Apply to icon-only button labels.
prefers-reduced-motionAll --motion-* tokens collapse to 1ms. No animation runs.
min touch target: 44×44pxbtn-icon-size = 2.75rem = 44px. btn-close--sm uses min-width/height to preserve visual density while meeting WCAG 2.5.5.
